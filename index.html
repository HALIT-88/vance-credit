No — and any company that tells you they can is violating federal law. We guarantee what we control: our documented effort, our dispute submissions, our follow-up. How credit bureaus respond is outside our control.

If after 6 months of active enrollment we have not submitted documented dispute activity on your credit file, we refund every service fee and work your file for another 6 months at no charge. This guarantees our effort — not specific outcomes.

How long does credit repair take?

Bureaus have 30 days by law to investigate each dispute. How long your journey takes depends on your specific file, the number of disputable items, and how bureaus respond. After reviewing your Vance Score Report, we give you an honest estimate.

Can I do this myself for free?

Yes — and we are legally required to tell you this, so we will. You have the right to dispute inaccurate items directly with the credit bureaus at no cost. That right is explained in the Consumer Credit File Rights disclosure you receive before signing anything with us.

Can I cancel?

Yes. Within 3 business days of signing — full refund, no questions. That's your right under federal law and it's in every Vance Credit contract. After that, cancel any time. You only pay for completed dispute rounds. No cancellation fees.

Do you work in every state?
